My Education

This web page outlines my educational background. It highlights my intellectual journey, and where I want to go from here

Pattonville Highschool

In Pattonville Highschool I spend most of my time either playing Volleyball or participating in speech and debate. I completed in many tournament, having success at the national level and performing well at many tournaments.

I was a good student, but I would not consider myself one of the top students in the school by any means.

Pattonville Highschool was very diverse and included students from diverse cultural and socioeconomic backgrounds.

Southwest Baptist University

Because of my success at Speech an Debate in highschool, I was offered a large scholarship to compete at Southwest Baptist University. I did that for four years while pursuing a double major of philosophy and biblical studies with a minor in ancient semitic languages. At this point, all I knew about my future was that I wanted to pursue more education.

So I began researching and applying to numerous universities. Many made my list, but I only completed applications for a few.

Ultimately, I settled on getting a master degree in communication from St. Louis University.
